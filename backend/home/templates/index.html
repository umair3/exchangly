FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get exchangly.com for free?

No, all the plans are paid and as a beginner you can start with our Lite plan. You can still explore our app using the demo login credentials. exchangly.com already provides premium quality service at a very competitive price.

What happens to unsubscribed emails?

When a user clicks on unsubscribe link, exchangly.com marks it as unsubscribed for you. He/She will not receive any future emails from your account.

Will my exchangly.com mails go to spam folder?

If you are sending too many emails beyond a certain threshold then a flag may be raised to put your mails in spam. exchangly.com has fully adapted email standards and do its best to avoid spaming emails. When you send email via exchangly.com, it is sent through your Integrated Sending Platform. If one person sends a spam, it might effect emails of other users.

What payment methods do you accept?

The Choose Plan option will redirect you to Payment Gateway page where you can choose the available payment options.
